Difference between cacao and cocoa

Cacao is the bean and cocoa is the powder made from it.

Cocoa Trees

Cocoa trees need to be planted next to taller trees to help protect them from too much sun.

Where they grow

Cocoa trees can in hot damp climates between the latitudes 20 degrees North and 20 degrees South.

Cocoa Tree Maturity

Cocoa trees take 3 to 5 years before it will bear fruit. Each tree will then bear around 1,000 fruit per year.
